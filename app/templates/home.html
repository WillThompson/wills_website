Who am I?

My name is Will. I am a data scientist and applied mathematician living in Vancouver, BC.

I obtained my Ph.D. at UBC where I studied the effects of random noise on nonlinear dynamical systems. The focus of my thesis was systematic dimensionality reduction methods for dynamical systems with multiple time scales and heavy-tailed stochastic driving. Here is a beautiful equation: $$ e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0.$$ I am a fellow at Insight Data Science where I worked on Patreon Pro, which offers recommendations for users for how to improve their Patreon campaign pages. I learned about natural language processing methods and setting up apps on Amazon Web Services over the course of this project.

I am a former compliance mathematician/statistician for an online gaming test laboratory, where I was responsible for analysing the mathematical and statistical character of games as well as the random number generators that serviced them.

For coding, I am a fan of python for its ease-of-use and its efficacy for data analysis and enjoy using flask, a python library for web development. In fact, it is running this little website on AWS. I am also experienced in R and, to a lesser extent, Java. Thanks for taking the time to visit!
