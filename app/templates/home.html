Who am I?

My name is Will. I am a Mathematician living in Vancouver, BC.

I obtained my Ph.D. at UBC in Applied Mathematics where I studied the effects of random noise on nonlinear dynamical systems. I have a real job now, but remain curious about various topics in math, statistics, and physics.

To balance out my academic credentials I have started taking an interest in coding and creating web-based tools. I am also fascinated with machine learning and data analysis, so I have begun to look deeper into this for personal and professional interest.

What is this?

To learn how to make some simple web applications, I had converted an old laptop into a little server. I had fun learning about how to setup websites and such, so I decided to get a more reliable computer so that I could explore this more fully.

I am a fan of python for its ease-of-use and its efficacy. Hence, I am using flask to code this little website. It is my intention that the things I write here can be of use for fun but maybe for something useful at some point.
