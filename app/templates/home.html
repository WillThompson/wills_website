Who am I?

My name is Will. I am a Mathematician living in Vancouver, BC.

I obtained my Ph.D. at UBC in Applied Mathematics where I studied the effects of noise (random effects) on nonlinear dynamical systems. I have a real job now, but remain curious about various topics in math, statistics, and science (primarily physics).

To balance out my academic credentials I have started taking an interest in coding (as many people in similar fields as me do) and creating web tools.

What is this?

To learn how to make some simple web applications, I have converted an old laptop into a little server.

I am a fan of python for its high ease-of-use to power ratio. Hence, I am using flask to code this little website. It is my intention that the things I write here can be of use for fun but maybe for something useful at some point.

For when I want to talk about math-related things, I will also publish some stuff here. Here is a beautiful equation: $$ e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$$
