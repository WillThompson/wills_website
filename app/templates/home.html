Who am I?

My name is Will. I am a data scientist and applied mathematician living in Vancouver, BC.

I obtained my Ph.D. at UBC where I studied the effects of random noise on nonlinear dynamical systems. The focus of my thesis was systematic dimensionality reduction methods for dynamical systems with multiple time scales and heavy-tailed stochastic driving.

I am a fellow at Insight Data Science where I worked on Patreon Pro, which offers recommendations for users for how to improve their Patreon campaign pages. I learned about natural language processing methods and setting up apps on Amazon Web Services over the course of this project.

I am a fan of python for its ease-of-use and its efficacy for data analysis and enjoy using flask, a python library for web development. In fact, I used it to code this little website. Before learning about the wonders of AWS, I converted an old laptop into a little server. I had so much fun learning about how to setup websites and little web applets that I decided to get a more reliable home computer that could function as a web server.

For when I want to talk about math-related things, I will also publish some stuff here. Here is a beautiful equation: $$ e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$$
