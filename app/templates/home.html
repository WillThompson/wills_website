Who am I?

My name is Will. I am a Data Scientist and Applied mathematician living in Vancouver, BC.

I obtained my Ph.D. at UBC where I studied the effects of random noise on nonlinear dynamical systems. The focus of my thesis was systematic dimensionality reduction methods for dynamical systems with multiple time scales and heavy-tailed stochastic driving.

I am a Fellow at Insight Data Science where I worked on Patreon Pro, which offers recommendations for users for how to improve their Patreon campaign pages. I learned about Natural Language Processing and setting up apps on AWS over the course of this project.

What is this?

Before learning about the wonders of AWS, I converted an old laptop into a little server. I had fun learning about how to setup websites and little web appliets, so I decided to get a more reliable computer so that I could explore this more fully.

I am a fan of python for its ease-of-use and its efficacy. Hence, I am using flask to code this little website. It is my intention that the things I write here can be of use for fun but maybe for something useful at some point.
